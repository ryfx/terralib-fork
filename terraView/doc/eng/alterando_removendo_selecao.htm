Changing and Removing Selection Color

The queried objects are identified by the differentiated colors of their lines in the table and their representation on the map. This color can be changed by the Visual > Query option from the theme popup menu.

The query can also be removed by turning the color of the objects back to its original color. Activate theme (1), use the popup menu and select the Remove Colors option (2) and next, Queried Objects (3).
