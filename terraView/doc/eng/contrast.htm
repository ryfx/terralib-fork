Image Enhancement

Usually a raw image has grey level grouped in a portion of the tone variation allowed by the sensor radiometric resolution (from 0 to 255 grey levels). The image is displayed with low contrast, hindering the distinction of targets in satellite images. This distinction is very important in image interpretation, since it improves the visual quality and emphasizes the characteristics of importance for a certain study. Contrast manipulation in a image fixes illumination effects, sensor defects or scene characteristics as imaged by the sensor.

First, in the view, keep only one raster image active, display it, and zoom.
