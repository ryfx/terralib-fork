Map Browsing

Data visualization scale may be changed to display with more detail regions of interest. A map can also be browsed. Use zoom buttons and the different cursor types in the tool bar to browse.

  • Click Zoom In button and click on the map to amplify the map two times.
  • Click Zoom Out button and click on the map to reduce the map two times.
  • Use Zoom Cursor to select an area to zoom in and click Draw.
  • Use Pan Cursor to move the map.
