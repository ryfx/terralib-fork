Themes Visualization of a View

 

A single View may contain many Themes, such as in the BH View case. In this example, various information about Belo Horizonte city, such as green areas, neighborhoods, PMMG (Police) battalions and companies, schools, location of crimes in year 2000, etc, are available.

When a view contains many themes, it is required to select only the themes of interest at that moment, in order to facilitate the visualization. To execute this, uncheck the themes that are unnecessary in the Views/Themes Tree. Follow the sequence of figures presented below, where visualization of a view themes has been disabled.

All visible Themes (the ones checked for visualizatioon) are displayed in the Draw Area when the Draw is clicked. They are drawn in the inverse order of their position in the Views Tree, from bottom to top, except for the active Theme, if it is not a raster type. In such case, the Theme will be drawn at last.
