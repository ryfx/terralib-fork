Changing Theme Visual


When right clicking on a theme, a popup menu will be activated offering a wide range of specific commands:

By visualizing the Figure above it is possible to verify the map of São Paulo Districts represented by its Planning Units. The setores View has as an active Theme the spatial locations and various geographic information, such as shelters, schools, etc. In the Visual option of the popup menu, the sub-option Default is used to change color, size and type of a geographic object, in order to visualize as desired.
