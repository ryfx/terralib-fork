Displaying Labels

A label with the value of an attribute associated to a geographic object may be displayed as the cursor moves on the map.

1. Activate the popup menu on POP2000 column header and choose Tooltip > ALL option.

After this, as the mouse moves on the map, the population of the neighborhood located at that point will be displayed.

2. To disable the display, activate the popup menu again on the header of POP2000 column and choose Tooltip > Hide option.

MIN, MAX, and COUNT options of the Tooltip menu are useful to handle dynamic tables.
