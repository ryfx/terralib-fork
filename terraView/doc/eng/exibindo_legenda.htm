Legend Display

Notice that legend categories data are shown in the Views tree under the associated theme. The legend can also be shown in the Draw area:

1. Activate the popup menu of Bairros theme.

2. Choose Visibility of Representation option.

3. In Theme Visibility interface, set Legends option.

The legend will be displayed on the upper left corner of the draw area. Click Enable Theme Edition button to move the legend to another location. After placing the legend at the desired position, click the button again to disable edition, avoiding unwanted modifications.

Remove Bairros2 theme by activating the popup menu and select Remove option.
