Creating Thematic Maps

Open BH.mdb database and activate Bairros theme. Observe that the information about a neighborhood name and its population in the year 2000 are available. To categorize the neighborhoods based on the number of inhabitants in the year 2000, execute the following steps.

1. Activate the popup menu of Bairros theme.

2. Select Edit Legend option.

Edit Legend window will be displayed for the categorization or grouping necessary to create thematic maps. Bar and Pie Chart will be presented in the next section.
